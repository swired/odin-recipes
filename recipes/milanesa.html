Milanesa

Description

Milanesa is a popular Argentine dish. You can think of milanesa as a cooking method, so it can be made with different types of meat or vegetables, such as beef, chicken, pork, or vegetables.

In this section, we will learn how to make a classic Argentine beef milanesa.

Ingredients

    1Kg of Eye of Round, Sirloin Tip or Bottom Round, thinly sliced
    A clove of garlic
    Some parsley
    Salt and pepper, to taste
    3 eggs
    breadcrumbs
    sunflower oil

Steps

    Chop the garlic and parsley leaves very finely and put them in a bowl.
    Crack the eggs into the bowl, season with salt and pepper, and mix well.
    Add the meat to the bowl and let it rest for at least half an hour so it absorbs the flavors.
    Coat the meat with breadcrumbs, pressing gently until it is fully covered
    Cook it in the oven at 180°C until it is golden, or fry it in oil.
